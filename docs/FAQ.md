# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is CoinVault?
CoinVault is a secure cryptocurrency investment platform that allows users to manage and grow their digital assets.

### Is CoinVault safe?
Yes. We implement industry-standard security measures including 2FA, encryption, and secure payment processing.

### What cryptocurrencies do you support?
We support major cryptocurrencies including BTC, ETH, USDT, and more.

## Account

### How do I create an account?
Visit [coinvalut.net](https://www.coinvalut.net), click "Register", and follow the signup process.

### How do I enable 2FA?
Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication and follow the setup instructions.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions to reset it.

## Deposits & Withdrawals

### What payment methods are accepted?
We accept cryptocurrency deposits and various fiat payment methods. Check the platform for current options.

### How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawal times vary by method. Crypto withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours.

### Are there withdrawal limits?
Yes, limits may apply based on your account verification level. Check your dashboard for details.
