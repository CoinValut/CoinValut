# How CoinVault Works

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account
Sign up at [coinvalut.net](https://www.coinvalut.net) with your email address. Verify your email to activate your account.

### Step 2: Secure Your Account
Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for enhanced security. We strongly recommend this step.

### Step 3: Make a Deposit
Fund your account using supported payment methods:
- Cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.)
- Supported fiat payment gateways

### Step 4: Start Investing
Browse available investment products and choose options that match your goals.

### Step 5: Track & Withdraw
Monitor your portfolio in real-time and withdraw your funds whenever you need.

## Investment Products

CoinVault offers various investment options to suit different risk profiles and investment goals. Visit our platform to explore current offerings.

## Fees

We believe in transparent pricing:
- **Deposits**: Varies by payment method
- **Withdrawals**: Network fees apply for crypto
- **Platform Fee**: Clearly displayed before each transaction
